NOTES FOR THE ONTOLOGY OF DIGITAL EDITIONS

There are components such as:

	Digital surrogates
	Transcriptions
	Modern critical editions
	Critical Paratexts
	Critical apparatus
	Prosopographies
	Bibliographies
	Gazetteers
	
There are continuums/spectrums such as:

	Single text - small collection - large anthology

	Single witness - multiple witnesses consulted - many witnesses transcribed/encoded

	Unannotated - editorial annotations only - editorial + external annotations

There are qualities such as:

	Peer-reviewed
	Open-access
	Open-source

There are output formats such as:

	XML code
	JSON
	HTML pages
	ePubs
	PDFs
