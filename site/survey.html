Endings Project Survey Results

Building Sustainable Digital Humanities Projects

The Project Endings global online survey was conducted between 2017 and 2018. The Call for Participation was sent out over DH-Listservs in English in 2017, and 127 respondants participated in the online form; of this group, respondants were given the opportunity to self-identify as being interested in an hour-long interview with more etailed questions. Of that group, 25 people from different DH projects were interviewed. The following anonymized results are from the online form only (original survey link). Any written answers have been ommitted to preserve anonymity.

Total Participants in the survey 127; no "required" answers.
