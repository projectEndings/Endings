Digital Humanities Quarterly Special Edition

Out of the 2021 Endings symposium will come a special issue of the Digital Humanities Quarterly. All our presenters will turn their presentations into full articles for the special edition, and an additional article focused on technical considerations and the software and site build processes developed by the Endings team will be contributed by Martin Holmes and Joey Takeda.

Please adhere to the journal submission guidelines when preparing your text. More information for authors will appear here in due course.
